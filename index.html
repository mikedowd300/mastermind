OBJECT

There are 4 randomly selected colors. You must figure out the color pattern, both by color and order, with a series of guesses.

STARTING

Each turn consists of selecting 4 colors(pegs) from the color-bar at the bottom and placing them into holes, starting at the bottom. Then clicking the "Take That" button, when it appears, in order to have your guess "graded". If you decide you don't like your color selection before hitting "Take That", you may replace the pegs the same way you originally selected them.

GRADING

Grading consists of white and black mini-pegs being placed to the right of your guess. A white mini-peg indicates that you have the correct color but in the wrong hole. A black peg indicates that you have placed the correct color in the correct hole.

There is exactly one mini-peg for each correct color guessed. There is no corrolation between the order of the mini-pegs and the order of the color pegs. The same color can be selected more than once.

STRATEGY

Use the grades from previous guessed to narrow down, and eventually figure out, the pattern.
